About Us

NTS was established as a project in 2002 which was part of the National Education Policy (1998-2010) and Information Technology (IT) Policy of the Govt. of Pakistan. Since its inception, NTS has always been proud to remain at the forefront of testing services in Pakistan.

With an unwavering commitment to excellence and integrity, NTS has become the nation's most trusted choice for conducting fair and transparent assessments.

Being Pakistan's inaugural pioneering testing service, NTS embodies the vision of advancing and elevating educational and professional testing standards.

NTS caters to diverse sectors, including education, recruitment, and professional development including trainings. With a proficient team and cutting-edge technology, NTS remains dedicated to enhancing and facilitating the admissions of talented students and managing human resources effectively in Pakistan and beyond.

- Who We Are

NTS is Pakistan's first, and self-sustained testing organization conducts tests and assessments for admissions, scholarships, recruitment and promotion purposes. It is governed by a Board of Directors of NTS.

+ VISION
